About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Test your skills, upgrade your character, and see how long you can survive in this challenging world full of danger and excitement.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby monsters and collect experience points to level up. Choose powerful upgrades to enhance your abilities and survive longer against increasingly difficult waves of monsters.

Endless Gameplay

Face wave after wave of monsters with increasing difficulty. How long can you survive?

Character Upgrades

Level up and choose from a variety of upgrades to enhance your character's abilities.

Diverse Monsters

Battle against a wide range of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.

Play Now

Game Tips

Focus on movement speed upgrades early to avoid getting surrounded. Prioritize damage upgrades to clear waves faster. Always keep moving to avoid getting cornered by monsters.
