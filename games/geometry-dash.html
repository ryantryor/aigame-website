About Geometry Dash

Geometry Dash is a fast-paced arcade platformer where you control a square and jump over obstacles to the rhythm of the music. Each level is filled with challenging jumps and hazards. Can you reach the end without crashing?

How to Play

Click or press the spacebar to jump. Time your jumps perfectly to avoid spikes and obstacles. The game gets faster and more challenging as you progress!

Rhythm-Based Gameplay

Jump and dodge obstacles to the beat of catchy music.

Challenging Levels

Test your reflexes and timing with increasingly difficult stages.

Addictive Fun

Easy to start, hard to master. Try to beat your high score!
