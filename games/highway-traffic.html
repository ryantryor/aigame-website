Highway Traffic

Race through busy highways, dodge traffic, and test your reflexes in this fast-paced driving game!

About Highway Traffic

Highway Traffic is an exciting driving game where you race down a busy highway, weaving between cars and trucks. The further you go, the faster the traffic gets! Collect points, avoid crashes, and see how long you can survive on the road.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your car left and right. Avoid hitting other vehicles and try to collect as many points as possible. The longer you survive, the higher your score!

Fast-Paced Action

Experience the thrill of high-speed driving and quick reflexes.

Endless Gameplay

Keep driving as long as you can without crashing to achieve the highest score.

Simple Controls

Easy to learn, hard to master. Perfect for quick gaming sessions.
