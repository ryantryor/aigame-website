About Solitaire

Solitaire is a classic card game that has entertained players for generations. The goal is to move all cards to the foundation piles, following suit and sequence. It's a perfect game for relaxing and sharpening your mind.

How to Play

Move cards between columns to uncover hidden cards and build sequences in descending order and alternating colors. Move cards to the foundation piles to win the game!
